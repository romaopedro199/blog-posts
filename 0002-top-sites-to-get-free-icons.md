# Top sites to get free icons

Icons are very useful and a visual way of helping people to understand your content. So, I have a list of the best websites I use to get free icons for my projects.

## Enjoy the list!

- [Font Awesome](https://fontawesome.com/icons?d=gallery)
- [Eva Icons](https://akveo.github.io/eva-icons/#/)
- [Icons8](https://icons8.com.br/)
- [Iconscout](https://iconscout.com/)
- [Remix Icon](https://remixicon.com/)
- [SVG Icons Sparkk](http://svgicons.sparkk.fr/)
